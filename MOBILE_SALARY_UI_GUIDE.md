## Responsive Behavior

### On Small Screens (< 400px)
- Font sizes: Reduce by ~5%
- Padding: Reduce by ~10%
- Card margins: Maintain 3px minimum
- Grid cols: Remain 2 columns where possible

### On Medium Screens (400px - 600px)
- Normal layout as designed
- All elements readable

### On Large Screens (> 600px)
- Content remains readable
- Padding increases for breathing room
- Bottom nav remains fixed
